# AirBlue — Travel Information (Baggage, Refunds, Exchanges, Liability)

## Reporting Time

Flights open for check-in 2.5 hours before scheduled departure on domestic flights and 4 hours before scheduled departure on international flights. Guests must check in 2 hours before flight departure. Check-in counters close 45 minutes before flight departure for domestic flights and 90 minutes before departure for international flights.

## Lost / Damaged Bag Compensation

The maximum liability for damaged or missing baggage is USD 20.00 per kg on international flights and PKR 500 per kg on domestic flights.

## Carry-On and Checked Baggage Allowance by Fare Type

### Value fare
- Hand Carry Bags: 7 Kg
- Checked Bags: Not Allowed
- Meals: Included
- Seat Selection: Preferred Seat
- BlueMiles Rewards: 50% Miles
- Refunds & Exchanges: Allowed with Higher Fee

### Flexi fare
- Hand Carry Bags: 7 Kg
- Checked Bags: 1 Bag, 20 Kg
- Meals: Included
- Seat Selection: Preferred Seat
- BlueMiles Rewards: 100% Miles
- Refunds & Exchanges: Allowed with Standard Fee

### Xtra fare
- Hand Carry Bags: 7 Kg
- Checked Bags: 1 Bag, 30 Kg
- Meals: Included
- Seat Selection: Preferred Seat (charges applied)
- BlueMiles Rewards: 200% Miles
- Refunds & Exchanges: Allowed with Lower Fee

**Carry-on limits:** 1 carry-on bag per guest; size limit 22in x 15in x 8in (L+W+H = 45 inches); weight limit 7 kg / 15 lb.
**Checked bag size limit:** L+H+W = 54 inches maximum per bag.

## Ticket Change

Guests may change the fare indicated on the e-ticket display, plus any difference in fare (the same fare is not guaranteed). Exchanges are allowed for the same sector only. Fees apply for each guest.

## Ticket Refund

Guests pay the refund fee indicated on the e-ticket display. For refunds, a National Identity Card (NIC) must be provided for all guests.

## Ticket Expiration

Tickets expire 30 days after the flight date. Expired tickets have no value and cannot be refunded, cancelled, or changed.

## Limited Liability

The maximum airline liability in the event of denied boarding, delayed, or cancelled flight is limited to the price paid for the ticket.
