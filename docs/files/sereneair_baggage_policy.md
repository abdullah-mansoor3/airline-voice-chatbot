## Baggage Dimensions

- Checked-in baggage dimensions should not exceed 54 inches (L+W+H=54).
- Hand carry should not exceed 7 kg in total with dimensions no more than 45 inches (L+W+H=45).
- Laptop will be excluded from hand carry.

## Lost or Mishandled Baggage — Claims Procedure

- Claims must be submitted on the "Baggage Claim Form" or the claim is inadmissible.
- **Missing/delayed baggage**: claim must be filed within 14 days from the date the baggage ought to have arrived. Compensation is paid per the Conditions of Carriage.
- Production of the baggage identification tag is essential for the claim.
- Serene Air assumes no liability for valuable items inside checked baggage.

**Procedure:**
1. Passenger must report at the Airport office and notify the complaint. If unresolved, the passenger is directed to the relevant sector representative.
2. Passenger must contact baggage services staff at the destination airport before leaving the arrival hall; staff will raise a "LOST or MISHANDLE" report.
3. Passenger must provide full name (correct spelling), any alternate name on the bag, permanent/temporary contact details, bag contents, and any other relevant information.
4. Passenger identifies the correct bag type/color via the identification chart provided by staff, and the bag's brand (e.g., Samsonite, Delsey).
5. Passenger should retain the baggage services contact numbers and claim file reference for further queries.
6. If the bag is untraced and the passenger must travel onward, they may contact baggage services staff at that point for further handling.
7. Further information: ll@sereneair.com

## Baggage Acceptance Policy / Prohibited Items

Carrying dangerous goods on board an aircraft is an offense. Spare lithium batteries for electronic devices (cameras, mobile phones, laptops) and other dangerous articles must not be carried in checked baggage. This is not a comprehensive list; passengers should consult Serene Air for details.
